vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known as Vijay or "Thalapathy" (Commander), is a leading Indian actor, singer, and politician in Tamil cinema, famous for commercially successful films like Ghilli, Thuppakki, and Leo, known for his action roles and dance moves, with a massive fan following, and recently launching his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). He debuted as a child actor in 1984 and as a lead in 1992, rising to become one of India's highest-paid stars with a career spanning over 30 years and nearly 70 films. Key Highlights
